Dreams 
Question 1: 
Can you remember the dreams you had? 
Useful phrases: 
vivid dreams 生动的梦 
fade quickly 很快消失 
strange details 奇怪的细节 
Sample answer: 
I can sometimes remember my dreams, especially when they are vivid or emotional. However, most of the 
time they fade quickly after I wake up, and only a few strange details stay in my mind. I usually forget them 
completely by the time I start my day. 
Question 2: 
Do you share your dreams with others? 
Useful phrases: 
close friends 亲密朋友 
keep it private 保持私密 
feel understood 被理解 
Sample answer: 
I don’t usually share my dreams with many people, only with close friends or family members. Some dreams 
feel too personal, so I prefer to keep them private unless I really trust someone. When I do share them, it 
makes me feel understood and supported. 
Question 3 
Do you think dreams have special meanings? 
Useful phrases: 
reflect emotions 反映情绪 
daily stress 日常压力 
not take seriously 不太当真 
Sample answer: 
I think dreams sometimes reflect emotions or daily stress rather than having special meanings. When I’m tired 
or worried, my dreams tend to be more intense or strange. Most of the time, though, I don’t take them too 
seriously and just see them as part of the brain resting. 
Question 4 
Do you want to make your dreams come true? 
Useful phrases: 
stay motivated 保持动力 
take small steps 迈出小步 
long-term goals 长期目标 
Sample answer: 
Yes, I definitely want to make my dreams come true, because having goals gives my life direction and meaning. 
I try to stay motivated by taking small steps every day instead of rushing or feeling pressured. Even if it takes 
time, working toward long-term goals makes me feel hopeful and confident.
